Prior to my PhD, I gained experience at the University of Texas at Tyler and industrial roles at ROMS Co., Ltd., Yamaha Robotics Holdings Co., Ltd., and Hiperdyne Corporation, where I applied my skills in Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Computer Vision to practical applications. I am passionate about using AI to solve challenging problems and contribute to the field.
